FAQ

  • Is the Free tier really free? Yes. The Free tier includes the full proxy, vault, compression, cost tracking, and all 9 client integrations — no trial period, no credit card.
  • Where is my data? Everything runs locally. TokenPak does not send your prompts, context, or metrics to any external server. Cost data is stored in a local SQLite file.
